Who can apply?

MYSP aims to prepare students for admissions abroad. Thus, MYSP will be most beneficial to high school students who are in 10-12th grade or who are graduating high school in 2022 and taking a gap year.

Qualifications

- Strong academic background

- Be interested in learning about studying abroad

- Involved in extra-curricular activities

- Not shy to speak English 24/7 :)

Application Process

FIRST: This round is an online application form comprised of short and long essay questions.

SECOND: After online application screening, we will notify the selected candidates over a phone call (or by email) and invite them for an short interview at Zorig Foundation.

If you have any questions, please contact Zorig Foundation.
