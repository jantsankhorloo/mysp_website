STUDENT APPLICATION FOR MYSP 2018 IS NOT OPEN YET.

The program is tentatively scheduled for August 9 - 16, 2018.

Who can apply?

MYSP aims to prepare students for admissions abroad. Students who are in their last 2 years of high school or who are graduating in May 2018 and/or taking a gap will benefit the most from this program.

Qualifications

- Strong academic background

- Desire to start his/her undergraduate studies abroad

- Involved in extra-curricular activities

- Not shy to speak English 24/7 :)

Application Process

FIRST: This round is an online application form comprised of short and long essay questions.

SECOND: After online application screening, we will notify the selected candidates over a phone call and invite them for an short interview at Zorig Foundation. The main purpose of the interview process is to get to know the candidate on a more personal level.

If you have any questions, please contact Zorig Foundation.
